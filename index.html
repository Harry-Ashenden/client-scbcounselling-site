About SCB Counselling

Sarah and her team have been providing schools with a bespoke counselling service for nearly 15 years. SCB Counselling is specifically tailored to students, staff, and families, and offers one-to-one counselling sessions within primary, secondary and PRU schools and organisations within the Southend area. SCB Counselling strives to provide the hope of a better future for children, in school and at home.

The team at SCB Counselling have a wealth of experience in working with students, staff, and families across the borough, offering them a safe space where they can talk about anything on their minds to an impartial listener. The counsellors have delivered crucial support to thousands of children that were in, often desperate need to have someone to talk to. With their ability, experience, and qualifications, they are able to develop therapeutic relationships with children that provide them with a safe, secure and confidential space that allows and encourages them to speak freely about their feelings and worries. The team have helped many students get back on track, staff cope with difficult situations, and families find their way through challenging times, and they'll do it with compassion and understanding.

What we Offer

SCB Counsellors, want your school, the children, the families, and the staff to thrive. The team offers a range of counselling services, all designed to provide tailored support. This includes one-to-one counselling with children, one-to-one sessions with parents, staff supervision, whole family support and child and parent counselling.

Why have a counsellor?

A school counsellor ensures that the children and staff have immediate access to support from a qualified professional providing a safe space to share anything, with the knowledge that it will be kept confidential and private (with the exception of safe-guarding issues).

Why choose SCB Counselling?

Schools trust SCB counsellors to provide their students with the care they need when they need it most. A school counsellor is a very privileged role, trusted with the emotional needs of often very vulnerable children, on a one-to-one basis. That's why all SCB counsellors are fully qualified, have a membership to the BACP or NCS, and are all DBS-checked and insured. It is imperative that any school that secures a counsellor, can completely trust who is working with their children.

SCB counsellors are also highly experienced, enabling them to meet the needs of every school that they are based in, adapting, and flexibly providing the support that is required.

If you feel that your school would benefit from an SCB Counsellor, get in touch with Sarah who will happily discuss your requirements, hours required, contract and costs.

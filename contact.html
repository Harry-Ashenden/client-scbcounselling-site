How it works

Once you get in touch with SCB Counselling we will assess your requirements and provide a tailor-made plan to suit your school's needs. These requirements can vary from how frequently our counsellor will be at the school, who they will be counselling, the type of counsellor required and more.

Once this is decided a contract will be written up and signed when agreed which will secure the counsellor until the academic year. If there are any issues or concerns that arise, SCB Counselling will be on hand to discuss these with you and come to a resolution.
